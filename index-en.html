Drupal 8 is here

Join developers, designers and drupalistas for two days of sessions, sprints,trainings and social activities.

Drupal Summer Barcelona is an event which aims to promote the use of Drupal and to interconnect businesses, agencies, developers, designers, project managers, users and other professionals.

Call for speakers

Call for speakers is now open! Do you want to share your knowledge about Drupal 8 or related topics? We're looking for amazing talks about backend and front end development but also site building, testing, business... If you have a great idea for a session, go ahead and get in touch with us. We're going to have a multi-lingual conference and proposals in Spanish, Catalan or English are very welcome!
